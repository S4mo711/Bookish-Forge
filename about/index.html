About Me

Hi, I’m Osama

I’m a freelance graphic designer with over six years of experience, having worked with restaurants, social media influencers, small businesses, and a variety of creative clients. Yet, it’s books that truly capture my heart. My passion for stories led me to specialize in book cover design—where I get to blend my love for art with the magic of storytelling.

Born and raised in Jordan and now based in Spain, I’ve been lucky enough to work with authors from different cultures, infusing my designs with a mix of vibrant tradition and modern flair. I believe every book deserves a cover that’s as unique and captivating as the story within it.

My Design Philosophy

Designing a book cover isn’t just about making something look good—it’s about creating a visual handshake between you and your reader. When we work together, it’s more than just a project; it’s a creative journey where your vision takes center stage.

I start by getting to know you and your book—your inspirations, your target audience, and even those little details that make your story one-of-a-kind. From there, I dive into brainstorming and sketching ideas, carefully crafting concepts that capture the essence of your narrative. We’ll refine every detail until the cover feels like it was made just for you..

Ready to give your book the cover it truly deserves? Let’s chat and create something extraordinary together.
